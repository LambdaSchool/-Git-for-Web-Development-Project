1. What is Semantic HTML? 
The way you markup a document to be interpreted by a web browser.

2. What is HTML used for? 
To tell a web browser how to display/interpret the webpage.

3. What is an attribute and where do we put it? 
They control content inside the tags behavior, and are declared on the opening tag.

4. What is the h1 tag used for? How many times should I use it on a page?
The biggest title tag, usually once for the title of the page.

7. What is an id?
